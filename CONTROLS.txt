Fist Fishing Controls:
---------------------------

W	-	forwards
A	-	right
D	-	left
SPACE	-	jump
Left Control	-	Descend
Left Shift	-	sprint
Z	-	target
X	-	forget target
Q	-	punch
M	-	mount boat
N	-	Dismount boat 
E	-	harvest/loot
P	-	throw bait
B	-	craft bait (while mounted on boat)
mouse	-	camera rotation
