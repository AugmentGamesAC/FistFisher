Fist Fishing Controls:
---------------------------


---------------------------
While Mounted on Boat:
---------------------------
W	Forwards
A	Left
D	Right
M	Map
N	Dismount Boat
I	Inventory
O	Shop Window


---------------------------
While In World:
---------------------------
W			Swim Forwards
RMB-Held	Rotate Camera
M			Mount Boat
I			Inventory
Z			Toggle Targetting
X			Change Target
Q			Punch
E			Harvest/Loot
P			Throw Bait
