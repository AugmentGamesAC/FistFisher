Fist Fisher Controls: Keyboard & Mouse
---------------------------
Space   -	start game
Escape	-	open pause/options menu

While on boat:
	W - forwards
	A/D - Rotate
	I - toggle between shop and map
	M - dismount
	
While in shop/Inventory: 
	Left-Click (& drag) - move items
	drag item between shop and inventory - buy/sell item (if possible)
	drag item out of valid space - throw away 

While Swimming:

	T - change camera modes
	
	Camera Mode 3rd person:
		W/S - Move Forwards/Backwards
		A/D - Strafe Left/Right 
		Q/E - Ascend/Descend 
		Mouse Move - Rotate Camera
		P - Punch (start combat)
		R - Harvest
	Camera Mode 1st person:
		W/S - Move Forwards/Backwards
		A/D - Strafe Left/Right 
		Q/E - Ascend/Descend 
		Mouse Move - Rotate Camera
		P - Punch (start combat)
		R - Harvest

While in Combat:
	O/P - change target
	F - punch/use attack
	W/A/S/D - open attack pinwheel (automatically uses one visibly selected)
