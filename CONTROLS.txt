
---------------------------------
Actions Available per context:
---------------------------------

While Travelling in Boat:
	Action (Dismount) (Button)
	MovementOrInventoryNavigation (steer boat) (3 Axis)
	DivingBellRaiseLower (Raise/Lower Diving Bell) (1 Axis) (Unimplemented)
	OpenInventory (Open Inventory/Shop) (Button)
	MainMenu (Pause/Show Main Menu) (Button)

While in the Inventory or Shop:
	MovementOrInventoryNavigation (selecting inventory slots) (3 Axis)
	Action (Buy/Sell/Eat) (Button)
	OpenInventory (Close inventory) (Button)
	MainMenu (Pause/Show Main Menu) (Button)
	HoldPickup (pick up selected item) (Button)
	MenuAndShopMenuNavigationPageSelect (Menu/Tab Navigation) (3 Axis)

While in Battle:
	Action (Attack) (Button)
	SecondaryAction (Use Bait) (Button)
	MainMenu (Pause/Show Main Menu) (Button)
	FleeOrCameraModeSwap (Flee) (Button)
	Targeting (Cycle though target fish) (1 Axis)
	AttackSwap (open pinwheel to select attack) (2 Axis)
	BaitSwap (open pinwheel to select bait) (2 Axis)

While Navigating Menus:
	Action (Select) (Button)
	SecondaryAction (Back) (Button)
	MainMenu (Pause/Show Main Menu) (Button)
	MenuAndShopMenuNavigationPageSelect (Menu/Tab Navigation) (3 Axis)

While Swimming:
	CameraRotation (Rotate your view around the player) (2 Axis)
	MovementOrInventoryNavigation (Movement) (3 Axis)
	Action (Mount, Harvest) (Button)
	SecondaryAction (Use Bait) (Button)
	OpenInventory (open inventory) (Button)
	MainMenu (Pause/Show Main Menu) (Button)
	FleeOrCameraModeSwap (Change bewteen 1st and 3rd person camera modes) (Button)
	BaitSwap (open pinwheel to select bait) (2 Axis)
